Homophobia, Transphobia, LGBTQ+🏳️‍🌈, Sexuality, some of us don't even know the real meaning of these words. Homophobia is the fear😱, hatred, discomfort, or mistrust of lesbian, gay, and bisexual people. Transphobia is the fear, hatred😡, despair with, or doubt against transgender, genderqueer, or don't follow traditional gender norms. LGBTQ+is used for lesbian👭, gay👨🏻‍🤝‍👨🏿, bisexual, transgender, queer, intersex and asexual people. Sexuality is about someone's sexual feelings, thoughts, attractions and behaviors towards other people.

Are we progressing as social beings👯‍♀️? Are we using our privileges for the benefit of our civilization? These are some questions❓that are yet to get an answer.
